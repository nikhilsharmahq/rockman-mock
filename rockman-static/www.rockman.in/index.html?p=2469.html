Overview

Our people are the greatest asset. They are at the heart of our ethos and human resources. The HR practices at Rockman follows the core philosophy of creating a high-performance culture with ample growth opportunities for self-development. The open yet challenging environment help our people realize their potential to excel in every sphere.

Our Values

  • Respect for individual
  • Integrity
  • Learning Organization
  • Customer Focus

We treat all people with fairness, dignity and respect. We respect privacy and provide a harassment-free environment where they can easily access mechanism for settlement. We don’t promote indiscrimination based on caste, creed, religion, gender, disability, age or any other characteristic and behaviour in a disrespectful, hostile, violent, intimidating, threatening or harassing manner.

We are open, honest, and ethical in all of our dealings. We honour all the commitments and comply with governing laws and statutory guidelines. Protecting sensitive information, maintain confidentiality and we deal fairly with customers, suppliers, and competitors.

Rockman provides its employees’ liberty to think and question existing systems, processes and practices. We encourage and support people to study and improve their skills and promote expertise. We invest in the continuous development of employees and motivate employees to generate ideas for improvement. We also support employees to experiment with new ideas – find ways to make things simpler, faster, more effective.

We ensure that customer requirements are identified and met. We respond to all customer enquiries promptly, positively and courteously and ask appropriate questions to identify customers needs. We always appreciate customer’s ideas and insist them to share ideas on ways to improve policies, procedures and service delivery.

Let’s Rock@Rockman

We believe that fun is an integral part of work and Rockman is abuzz with activities of various nature keeping the employees engaged throughout the year. The yearly family fest – “Family Mela” is a platform which allows employees to showcase their inherent talent in music, dance, theatre, etc. and also brings in a family of employees at one platform to have a colour of festivities. The enthusiasm and passion of kid are reflected by the employees when they play, football, table tennis, volleyball and cricket.
